Blake Holman

I'm Blake, a senior in Computer Science at the University of Texas at Austin (Class of 2021)

I am broadly interested in theoretical computer science, especially algorithmic game theory and cryptography. My recent work involves characterizing the popularity criterion in the context of Three-Dimensional Stable Marriage.

I also conduct research with the Building-Wide Intelligence Project with Dr. Justin Hart. Here, I gained experience in robotics, localization, navigation, perception, and manipulation working on numerous projects over the years.
